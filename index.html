Step 2: Install Dependencies

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes MOG AutoClicker different?
MOG AutoClicker stands out with its combination of high performance, advanced features, and user-friendly interface. Our click randomization and custom hotkey system provide unmatched flexibility.
Is it safe to use?
Yes, MOG AutoClicker is completely safe and open-source. You can review the code on our GitHub repository. However, always check the rules and policies of the applications where you plan to use it.
What's the maximum CPS?
MOG AutoClicker can achieve up to 10,000 CPS (Clicks Per Second), though we recommend using lower values for most applications to ensure stability and prevent system strain.
